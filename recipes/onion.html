CARAMELIZED ONION DIP

This dip was a perfect appetizer for company!

Combining everyone’s favorite condiments into one seriously savory umami-bomb takes minimal effort, but yields maximum reward.

This creamy, crunchy, tangy dip will have your guests begging for it’s return at the next tailgate or picnic.

Ingredients

Everything Seasoning

  • 1 tablespoon white sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons black sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons poppy seeds
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon flaky sea salt, such as Maldon

Caramelized Onions

  • 2 medium yellow onions, sliced (about 4 cups)
  • Canola oil (or other neutral oil)
  • Kosher salt

For The Dip

  • 1 cup (8 ounces) cream cheese
  • ¼ cup (65g) crème fraîche
  • 1 tablespoon (17ml) buttermilk
  • ½ cup (130g) caramelized onions, finely chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons Everything Seasoning, plus more for sprinkling
  • Kosher salt
  • Chives, sliced

To Serve

  • Ridged potato chips
  • Cucumber slices or strips

Cooking Instructions

  1. Everything Seasoning: Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and taste, adjust seasonings to your liking. Mix can be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 months.
  2. Caramelize the onions: Heat about 1 tablespoon of canola oil in a large skillet or dutch oven over medium-low heat.
    Once hot, add the onions and stir to coat. Let cook, stirring every 5-10 minutes, until deeply caramelized, about 1 ½ - 2 hours.
    If the onions start to burn at any point, lower the heat slightly and add a splash of water to deglaze any bits stuck to the pan.
    Season with salt and set aside to cool completely.
  3. For the Dip: Place cream cheese in a medium bowl and whip with a hand mixer until light and fluffy.
    Add in creme fraiche and buttermilk, whip again until fluffy. Add caramelized onions, everything seasoning, and a pinch of salt and whip again.
    Taste and adjust seasoning as needed before transferring to a serving bowl and topping with more everything seasoning and chives.
    Excellent served alongside vegetables or pita chips.
